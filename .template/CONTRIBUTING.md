# Contributing to My Vibe Coding Template

Thank you for your interest in this project! 🙏

## 📢 Important Notice

This is a **personal template repository** designed for my own workflow and preferences. While I appreciate the community's interest, **I am not accepting pull requests at this time**.

## ✅ What You Can Do

### 🐛 Report Issues
If you find a bug, typo, or have a suggestion, please **open an issue**:
- [Open an Issue](https://github.com/matheme-justyn/my-vibe-scaffolding/issues/new/choose)
- Use the appropriate template (Bug Report or Feature Request)
- Provide clear description and context

### 🔄 Fork and Customize
You're encouraged to:
- **Clone** this repository
- **Fork** it for your own use
- **Customize** it to fit your needs
- **Share** your own version with others

This template is released under the [MIT License](./LICENSE), so you're free to use and modify it for your own projects.

### 💬 Discuss
- Share your experience using this template
- Ask questions in the [Issues](https://github.com/matheme-justyn/my-vibe-scaffolding/issues)
- Suggest improvements or new ideas

## ❌ What I'm Not Accepting

- **Pull Requests**: I'm maintaining this template according to my personal preferences and workflow
- **Feature Requests**: While I appreciate suggestions, I may not implement them to keep the template aligned with my needs

## 🤔 Why No Pull Requests?

This template reflects my **personal development philosophy and workflow**. Accepting external contributions could dilute the opinionated nature that makes it useful for my specific needs.

However, this doesn't mean your ideas aren't valuable! I encourage you to:
- Create your own version based on this template
- Share your customizations with the community
- Build upon these ideas in your own way

## 📝 Questions?

If you have questions about:
- How to use this template
- Why certain decisions were made
- How to customize it for your needs

Please feel free to **open an issue** and I'll do my best to help!

---

**Remember**: This template is a starting point, not a destination. Feel free to adapt it to your own "vibe"! 🎨
